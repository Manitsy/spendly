Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we collect the expense data you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what you explicitly provide.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and powering the features you use.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the core service. We will never sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

You may delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove all associated data from our systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. This may result in a request to Google's servers when you visit the site. No personal or expense data is shared with Google or any other third party.

We do not use analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking pixels.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly.
